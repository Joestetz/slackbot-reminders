About

Learn about this little tool and how to use it.

Who or what is Slackbot?

Slackbot is Slack's built-in robot. A key capability offered by Slackbot is to remotely post to a team's channels via HTTP POST. To read more about it, you can visit this post on the sick site .

How do I use this tool?

The sole purpose of this tool is to configure automatic messages for your team's channel(s). Currently supported are one-time messages based on a date and time and repeating messages on a given day of the week at a specific time.

To get started, you will need to enable the slackbot integration on your site. Once enabled, there will be a token available specifically for this integration that you will need to copy and paste into the home screen of this site. You will also need your team name (subdomain of your URL such as https://SUBDOMAIN.slack.com).

Once we have those two bits of information, you can manage your existing team reminders and create new ones.
